Welcome

Welcome to Club Cybersecurity at JMU. This webpage is dedicated towards supplying information and details about our club to any student who may be interested. We are a privately funded club at JMU dedicated towards helping students learn and participate in cybersecurity events.

About Us

We were established in 2017 and were created by students just like yourselfs. We are aimed towards helping students gain experience in things such as programming, penetration testing, and blue team skills.
